Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

•Total Time: Approximately 10 minutes

•Prep Time: 5 minutes

•Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients:

• large eggs

• Salt to taste

• Pepper to taste

• 1 tablespoon butter or oil

• Optional fillings: Cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, or herbs

Instructions:

1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined.
You can add a splash of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and melt the butter or add oil.

3. Cook the eggs : Pour the beaten eggs into the pan, tilting it to spread them evenly.

4. Add fillings: When the edges start to set but the center is still slightly runny,
add your chosen fillings.

5. Fold and finish: Carefully fold the omelette in half and let it cook for another minute until fully set

Nutrition Information (per serving, without fillings):

Calories 270kcal
